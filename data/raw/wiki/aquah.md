Aquah is the school of water magic, drawing on rivers, oceans, rain, ice, and the moisture in the air. Aquah witches are typically refined, controlled, and a little reserved — water rewards patience and punishes carelessness, and the school's culture has internalised the lesson.

The Aquah palette is blues and silvers, with flowing fabrics and jewellery shaped like waves, fish, or pearls. Aquah witches often live near the sea or on lakes, in elegant homes that look slightly too clean to be lived in. Their magic ranges from gentle (calming a storm, conjuring drinkable water) to spectacular (raising a wave, freezing a lake solid in seconds).

Saffron, one of the young witches who appears alongside Pepper in the comic, belongs to Aquah. She is poised, confident, and cordially competitive, holding herself to a high standard of magical refinement that the rest of the young witches find both impressive and exhausting.

Aquah witches view themselves as the most disciplined of the four schools and the others mostly let them have it.
