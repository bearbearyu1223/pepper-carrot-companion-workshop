Hereva is the world in which the webcomic *Pepper&Carrot* takes place. It is a continent of forests, oceans, mountains, and chaos-blasted plains, populated by witches, magical creatures, and the occasional ordinary mushroom-picker who wandered too far from their village.

Magic on Hereva is organised into four traditions, each with its own school, goddess, and aesthetic: Chaosah (chaos), Hippiah (nature), Magmah (fire and lava), and Aquah (water). Most witches are trained in one school from a young age, though rivalries between the schools are gentle by tradition and merciless in practice.

The world is shaped by gods who occasionally take a personal interest in mortal affairs — sometimes helpfully, sometimes not. There is no single overarching empire; villages, witch-houses, and small wandering communities are the dominant social units. Travel between regions is mostly on foot, broom, or the back of a polite magical creature.

David Revoy created Hereva as the setting for *Pepper&Carrot* and has expanded it through episodes and side material. The lore is canon-light and friendly to fan contributions; the wiki at peppercarrot.com is community-maintained.
