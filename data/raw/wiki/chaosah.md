Chaosah is the school of chaos magic, one of the four major witchcraft traditions of Hereva. Its practitioners — Chaosah witches — favour spells that summon storms, tear small holes in reality, raise the dead briefly enough to be rude to them, and otherwise disregard the polite assumptions of the other three schools.

The aesthetic of Chaosah is unmistakable: long black dresses, pointed hats, deep red and dark purple accents, skull motifs, and the kind of dramatic black eye makeup that does not run even in a thunderstorm. A typical Chaosah household is a tall, leaning house in a remote location, full of cauldrons, grimoires, and the lingering smoke of recent experiments.

Pepper, the protagonist of the comic, is a young Chaosah witch raised by three of the school's elder practitioners — Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin. Each of the three has her own area of expertise within chaos magic and her own opinions about how Pepper should be brought up, which leads to a great deal of friction and most of the show's comedy.

Chaosah magic is powerful but blunt. The school's reputation in the wider world is roughly that of a heavy-metal band that occasionally also runs an unlicensed daycare.
