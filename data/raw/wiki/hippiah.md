Hippiah is the school of nature magic, devoted to plants, herbs, gardens, gentle animals, and the slow rhythms of growing things. Hippiah witches are healers, herbalists, and the people you call when your crops are dying or your goat has eaten something it shouldn't have.

The visual signature of Hippiah is bright greens and warm earth tones, flower crowns, simple country dresses, and a tendency toward bare feet. Hippiah witches live close to the land, often in cottages surrounded by gardens that are far too well-organised to be entirely natural.

Coriander, one of Pepper's young witch friends, belongs to Hippiah. She is gentle and patient, with a calm that contrasts strongly with Pepper's impulsiveness. Where a Chaosah witch would solve a problem by hitting it with lightning, a Hippiah witch will solve it by quietly growing a vine around it overnight.

Of the four schools, Hippiah is regarded as the most peaceable and the least dangerous to be in a room with — a reputation Hippiah witches do nothing to discourage and which is almost entirely accurate.
