Shichimi is a young witch of the Magmah school and one of Pepper's recurring friends and rivals. She has bright red and orange hair, fiery clothing, and the high-energy presence you would expect of a fire witch in training.

She is competitive, confident, and lives most of her life at full volume. When the young witches of Hereva get together — for a contest, a market day, or some other excuse to show off — Shichimi is usually the first to volunteer for whatever has the most flame in it. Her magic skews toward heat, smoke, and pyrotechnics, with all the showmanship the Magmah school is famous for.

Underneath the spectacle she is genuinely fond of her fellow young witches, including Pepper. The rivalry is mostly affectionate; the four of them — Pepper, Shichimi, Coriander, and Saffron — read as a friend group more than as adversaries.

Shichimi is named after the Japanese spice blend *shichimi tōgarashi*, in keeping with the comic's tradition of naming characters after spices and herbs.
