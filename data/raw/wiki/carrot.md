Carrot is Pepper's cat: a small, bright orange tabby with a white belly, large green eyes, and the dignified expression of a creature who has seen all of his witch's experiments and survived most of them. He is named for his colour rather than for any particular fondness for vegetables.

Carrot is Pepper's familiar in the everyday rather than the strictly magical sense. He follows her everywhere, including into ill-advised laboratories, sleeps on whichever potion bottle is warmest, and reacts to chaos magic with the long-suffering equanimity of a cat who has been blown up before and knows it generally wears off.

He is intelligent, communicative through eloquent body language, and frequently the only character in the room with a sensible opinion about what should happen next. The audience is meant to read his expressions like subtitles for the scene.

Carrot does not speak, but his role in the comic is closer to a co-protagonist than a pet. Many panels are framed entirely around his reaction to whatever Pepper has just done.
