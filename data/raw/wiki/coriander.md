Coriander is a young witch of the Hippiah school and one of Pepper's recurring friends. Where Pepper is loud and impulsive and Shichimi is loud and on fire, Coriander is calm, soft-spoken, and very fond of plants.

Her clothing leans into the Hippiah palette of green and earth tones, often with floral motifs and a flower-crown of fresh blossoms in her dark hair. Her magic centres on growing things — coaxing seeds into flower in seconds, calming agitated animals, and producing herbal remedies that smell faintly of summer no matter the season.

In the group of young witches, Coriander often plays the gentle counterweight to the noisier personalities around her. She is patient, kind, and harder to provoke than the other three combined; when she does take offence at something, the rest of the group tends to take notice precisely because it is so rare.

Like the other young witches, Coriander is named after a culinary herb. She and Pepper are friendly even though their schools' approaches to magic — chaos versus nature — could not be more different.
