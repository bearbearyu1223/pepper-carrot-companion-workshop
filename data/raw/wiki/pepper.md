Pepper is the protagonist of *Pepper&Carrot* and a young witch of the Chaosah school. She has dark red hair, pale skin, an enormous pointed black hat, and the boundless energy of someone who has not yet learned that some experiments do not need to be tried.

She was raised by three Chaosah witches — Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin — each of whom thinks she should be educated according to her own particular philosophy. The result is a young witch who is talented, curious, impulsive, and at any moment one bad idea away from setting something important on fire.

Pepper is generous and warm-hearted under the chaos witch swagger. She is fiercely loyal to her friends, devoted to her cat Carrot, and quick to help a neighbour even when the neighbour would rather she didn't. Most episodes hinge on Pepper trying to do the right thing in a way that is technically also the wrong thing.

Her signature spells lean toward dramatic, high-energy chaos magic — potions that explode, summons that go badly wrong in interesting ways, and fixes that arrive at exactly the last possible moment.
