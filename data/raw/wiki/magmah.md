Magmah is the school of fire and lava magic. Its witches draw their power from heat, flame, volcanic vents, and the deep furnaces beneath Hereva's mountains. They are passionate, loud, and slightly singed around the edges.

A Magmah witch's wardrobe runs to oranges, reds, and golds, often with metal jewellery that glows faintly in the dark. Their houses tend to be near volcanoes, hot springs, or — failing that — kitchens with industrial-grade stoves. Cooking, smithing, and metalwork are common Magmah specialities alongside the more obvious offensive fire magic.

Shichimi, one of Pepper's young witch friends, belongs to Magmah. She is energetic and competitive, never happier than when there is something to be on fire about. Like all Magmah witches she carries herself with the confidence of someone who genuinely believes most problems can be solved by applying enough heat.

Magmah magic is showy and fun to watch. It is also the most likely of the four schools to set the audience on fire, which is part of the appeal.
