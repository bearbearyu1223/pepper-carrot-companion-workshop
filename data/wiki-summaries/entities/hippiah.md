Hippiah is the school of plants, creatures, insects, and all living things. It sits at the top-middle of the Impossible Triangle as the central sub-school of Evolutions magic, and is the most highly regarded school of Evolutions because it is the most willing to share its knowledge. Hippiah documents farming, gardening, and fertility in great depth, serving as a guide for most of the farmers of Hereva.

Hippiah was founded long ago by a witch named Dandelion, who compiled the oral traditions of her predecessors into a single volume so they would not be lost. Demand for copies grew so heavy that other witches helped transcribe and distribute them, and the school formed around that work. The school's deeper spells remain restricted to trained adepts because uncontrolled growth magic can go badly wrong. Many doctors in Hereva still use diluted Hippiah recipes in their practice, and a thriving business pre-mixes those recipes for them.

During the Great War Hippiah played a support role rather than fighting, scouring battlefields for the wounded and growing provisions for both sides. Hippiah witches were the ones who, after the destruction of Chaosah, escorted Zombiah to revive the surviving Chaosah witches and restore the magical balance of Hereva. Today the School of Hippiah is a modest U-shaped building on the outskirts of Squirrel's End, run by Basilic with a more traditional and demanding style than her predecessor. Pepper studied here briefly before her aptitude for chaos magic became unmistakable. Hippiah's quiet patrols still bring healing to the lost and wounded, and the school continues to teach farmers across Hereva, though enrollment has dwindled since the rise in popularity of Magmah. As Pepper relays, Hippiah is not a magic for making weedkillers.
