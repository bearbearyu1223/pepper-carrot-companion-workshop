Komona is the flying city built around the base of the Great Tree of Komona. The tree's rounded branches shade most of the city and its roots support a near-perfect sphere. Despite its open appearance Komona is well protected; every visitor passes through the gate under the watchful eyes of the Komonan Guard. Property in Komona is expensive even for a tiny office space, and residents are rich and upper-class.

Komona's wealth comes from the weekly markets, where vendors and travelers from across Hereva trade food, magical artifacts, spells, and Komonan wood. The Great Tree itself is the most revered tree on Hereva; many believe it mirrors the health of magic in the world, and a branch of Komonan botanists samples its leaves to measure the flow of Rea. Pure Komonan-wood brooms can fetch eye-watering prices and are sold exclusively at auction; blends are common, certified by specialist appraisers.

Because Komona floats above the lands of King Acren, it believes it is outside his taxation and rules. Acren's waning influence and the lack of an airborne army leave the standoff in place. Komonan leisure activities range from the meditative pruning of the Great Tree to the occasional spectacular accident when an apprentice gardener miscuts and is tossed off the platform by a rapidly regrowing branch. The Mayor of Komona, currently Mayor Bramble, governs from the only high office in the city.

Komona hosts the major magical contests of Hereva. The first Potion Contest devastated the market square so thoroughly that the City Elders moved future contests to a purpose-built arena. Saffron lives in the heart of the city and runs her magic consultancy from its office district. Above the city floats Kerberos, a satellite city formed when the Great Tree shed a limb after the destruction of Chaosah during the Great War.
