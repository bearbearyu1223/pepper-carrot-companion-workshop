Hornuk is a Chaosah demon with green fur and two prominent horns on his head. He is a fierce fighter who uses claws and horns to gore opponents and a gigantic maw to consume them; whatever he swallows is slowly digested over hundreds of years. He is not as magically gifted as the other Chaosah demons, but he does not need to be; he will relentlessly pursue his opponents until they either give up or are destroyed. Like the other demons of Chaosah he dwells in a dimension outside Hereva and can be summoned to the main reality only briefly.
