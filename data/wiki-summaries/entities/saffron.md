Saffron is a young Magmah witch of about eighteen and Pepper's frequent rival, sharing the same generation as Pepper, Coriander, and Shichimi. She lives in the heart of Komona, the flying city, where she runs a thriving magic consultancy called Saffron Witchcraft in the highest-rent district of the markets. Her familiar is Truffel, a white angora cat.

Saffron demonstrated an aptitude for magic at an early age. At six she managed to counterfeit a handful of Ko so closely that even the head of the mint could barely tell the difference. Rather than punish her, the authorities sent her to the advanced Magmah classes, where she quickly realized she was pages ahead of her teachers. She left formal education to open her own business, and her clientele today ranges from Komonan merchants who want their wares improved to Zombiah alchemists researching new materials. The Ko mint itself has consulted her on harder-to-counterfeit coin designs.

Her ambition is driven by the desire to be the best witch and to restore the prestige of Magmah, which she sees as the school of urban polish: metal, gems, forge, glass, mirrors, shining rocks, and caves. She and Pepper trade victories and barbs across the comic; her known spells include the Potion of Poshness, which she once poured on a recently zombified canary at the first potion contest, and a straightforward fire spell. Despite the rivalry the four young witches read as a friend group as often as adversaries, and Pepper has more than once gone to Saffron for help when life at the Chaosah house became unworkable.
