Yuzu is Shichimi's familiar, a young two-tailed orange kitsune who travels beside Shichimi through the nomadic territory of Ah in the Land of the Setting Moons. His hobbies are sleeping and grooming, and his motivation is loyalty to Shichimi and protecting her should she be in danger. Like the other familiars in the comic he does not speak and has no known magic of his own, but his role at Shichimi's side is constant: where she goes, he goes.
