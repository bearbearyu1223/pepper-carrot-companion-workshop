Theorem the Golem is part of Pepper's security system, living in the mountains behind Squirrel's End. Most of the time he sits in quiet contemplation, so still that grass, vines, and moss have grown on him and pigeons have built a nest on his left arm. When an intruder flies by he springs into action, using a comically long ball-and-chain to keep his prisoners from getting too far away. He takes great pride in presenting his unwitting captives to Pepper when she remembers to collect them. Pepper recruited him after mistaking him for a statue while placing trap elements around him.
