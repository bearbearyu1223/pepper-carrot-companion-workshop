Spider-Admins are large spiders that administer Hereva's Crystal Ball Network from caverns in the forests of Hereva. The network provides news, videos, entertainment, and gossip to those who can afford a crystal ball or visit a public access terminal. Spider-Admins make sure the network is operational and consult with anyone wanting to build sites on it. They can seem intimidating at first because of their size, but they are more interested in helping folks enjoy the network than in scaring them. Spider-Admins prefer the glow of crystal balls to outdoor light and rarely leave their caves; Thyme has her own Crystal Ball Terminal in one of their caves for the fastest connection available.
