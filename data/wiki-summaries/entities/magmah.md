Magmah is the school of cooking, baking, grilling, boiling, frying, steaming, and toasting, and also the house of alchemy and rare metals. It sits at the middle-right of the Impossible Triangle and is a major school. Magmah witches use a blend of Hippiah and Magmah magic to create amazing dishes, and supply strong materials to Zombiah for its automatons. Most folks on Hereva know a diluted form of Magmah, but the gap between a dabbler and an adept is enormous.

The school was founded by three witches who ran a catering business: Savory, Mint, and Chive. Their magic focused on heating and combining materials into something new, which they extended into crafting cooking utensils and exquisite one-of-a-kind place settings. Demand outstripped what three witches could handle, so Heliantha was brought in to found and run the school while the three founders kept the catering business going. Magmah's wealth and Komona's wealth grew in lockstep, and the school is closely tied to the floating city to this day.

Magmah and Aquah started the Great War over a series of escalating grievances about weather patterns and drained lakes. As part of the treaty Aquah renamed Magmah "Kielbasah" for ten years as punishment, and the spell was so powerful that even casual misspellings were corrected to the new name. After the war Magmah rebuilt the quickest of the six schools, with the markets of Komona springing up around the wounded Great Tree. The current leader is Vanilla, a former student of Heliantha; her most famous witch is Saffron, who has bested every other Magmah witch in contest and consults from her own office in Komona. Modern Magmah leans toward image and style, with applicants paying 5,000 Ko nonrefundable just to join the waiting list.
