Cumin is the kindly bon-vivant witch of Chaosah and Pepper's favorite of the three godmothers. She is in her late sixties and specializes in creating potions and in studying the various plants of Hereva she uses as their ingredients. Her potions are potent and she is happy to share her knowledge; some Chaosah scholars credit her approachability for Pepper's preference for potion-craft over other styles of incantation.

Cumin is Thyme's apprentice, and despite being an accomplished witch in her own right she still suffers under the apprentice relationship, being bossed around almost instinctively by the older witch. She is only somewhat aware of how strained the relationship has become; she grew suspicious after an errand to Aquah where she nearly drowned because Thyme neglected to mention how far underwater the school is, but she has not yet put the pattern together. Her known spells include transforming herself into a bat and invoking a tornado butterfly.

Cumin is also an excellent cook whose dishes rival those of Magmah witches. She tastes flavors with wild abandon and can usually be found near the food at any gathering, sampling everything in sight and quietly drafting new ideas for her own kitchen. She was one of three Chaosah witches resurrected by Zombiah after the Great War with the standing condition that she train an heir; Pepper is that heir. One piece of Chaosah wisdom Pepper relays from her is that to know the edible plants is to know where to find ready-made potions against hunger.
