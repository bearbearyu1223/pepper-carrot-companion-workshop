Hereva is the magical world Pepper and Carrot inhabit, born of the cosmic forces known as Chaos and Evolutions. Hereva's magical nature permeates it so completely that any practitioner can find the resources to manipulate magic, though specialized training is required to channel that energy properly. Three moons orbit the planet, simply called the Three Moons of Hereva, all visible during Pink Moon. Bergamot's Teapot orbits opposite the moons and can be seen by careful observers at moonrise.

Hereva's geography resists mapping. Few places have consistent locations; Ah, for instance, is always found where the three moons set. Travelers between landmark cities like Komona and Qualicity follow magical beacons, and a thriving cartography business sells subscriptions to self-updating maps because the terrain shifts often enough to defeat fixed charts. Many legends compete for the story of Hereva's creation; some claim it formed from the dust of another magical world, others that it grew out of dragon-fire.

Hereva's six schools of magic divide between Chaos (Chaosah alone) and Evolutions (Hippiah, Magmah, Aquah, Zombiah, and Ah). They are arranged on the Impossible Triangle, which Wasabi of Ah controversially reframed as a unity pyramid that places Ah at the top. Magic on Hereva flows in a substance called Rea, which fills the world and is concentrated in places like the Great Tree of Komona. Magical creatures range from peaceful DragonCows over Squirrel's End to fearsome dragons and the Chaosah demons summoned from a dimension outside Hereva's main reality. Hereva's weather is surprisingly predictable, governed in large part by Aquah; weather-prediction agencies have grown so accurate that they can detect when amateurs are tampering with the storms.

The Great War shook the entire world. When Chaosah was destroyed the Great Tree of Komona ruptured and the city began to fall; the war ended only after Zombiah resurrected enough Chaosah witches to restore the balance of magic.
