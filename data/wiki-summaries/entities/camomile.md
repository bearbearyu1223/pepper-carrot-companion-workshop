Camomile is a young Hippiah witch from Pepper's old class at the School of Hippiah Witchcraft in Squirrel's End. She is about eighteen and is human with raccoon ears and a tail. Early in school she was teased for her appearance, but her gifts with Hippiah magic quickly endeared her to her teachers and classmates. She is driven to be the best Hippiah witch ever, and her formidable talents make that ambition plausible. Her familiar Squeak is a squirrel who lives under her hat. Her known spell is a growth spell she used in episodes 18 and 22.
