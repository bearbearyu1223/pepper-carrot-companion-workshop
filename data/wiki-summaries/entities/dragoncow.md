DragonCows are docile dragon-bovine hybrids that congregate over the forests of Squirrel's End, spending most of their days chewing trees and calmly gliding through Hereva's skies. They are mostly left alone because of their size and because mature DragonCows have been known to kick opponents with enough force to break the sound barrier. They are prized for their milk, which some find tastier than ordinary cow's milk, and they are far more intelligent than their lazy demeanor suggests, capable of critical thinking and complex problem solving. Their main drawback is a clumsy take-off and landing that leaves a trail of broken trees and ruts in the ground.
