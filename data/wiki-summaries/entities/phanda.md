The Phanda is a large, furry creature with the face and trunk of an elephant and the body of a badger. Its back is lined with horns and it lives in the forests and jungles of Hereva, where its exact range is unknown. The Phanda is considered quite dangerous, especially to unwary travelers; it is the kind of creature Squirrel's End villagers stay out of the deep forest to avoid.
