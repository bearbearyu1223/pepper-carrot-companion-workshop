The Savant-Ants are a colony of ants near Pepper's house in Squirrel's End who were magically uplifted to genius-level intelligence by one of Pepper's potions of genius. She thought the potion had failed after testing it on Carrot and threw it out the window onto the unsuspecting colony. The ants have since taken up mathematics and space travel, and they share with all academics an incessant need to mention their advanced degrees. Some may be sneaking into the Chaosah house to read the tomes in the hope of uncovering more secret knowledge for their own experiments.
