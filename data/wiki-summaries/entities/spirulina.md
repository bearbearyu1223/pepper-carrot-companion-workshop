Spirulina is the mysterious witch-representative of Aquah who appeared at the second magic contest in Komona, much to the surprise of Pepper and the audience. She is about fourteen and her familiar is Durian, a male betta fish. Few have seen members of Aquah since the Great War, so little is known about her motivation or what Aquah hopes to gain by sending her. Within Aquah she is the one who unearthed a cache of written spells and documents that had been set aside in favor of telepathic teaching, and the rediscovery has revived spells the school had nearly forgotten. Her known spells include forceful ejection from water and Water Tentacles.
