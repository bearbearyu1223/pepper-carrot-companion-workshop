The Dragonmoose is a dragon-moose hybrid that emerged from the magical explosion that accompanied the collapse of the Great Tree of Komona. Moose are not native to Hereva, which has fueled the inter-dimensional theorists' argument that Hereva is connected to other universes, and the inter-dimensional skeptics' counter that no universe could contain something as ridiculous as a moose. Dragonmoose nest in the mountains behind Squirrel's End and have eggs of bright colors. Most observers who try to study them find themselves plucked from their vantage point and placed gently into the nest, where a bemused dragonmoose nuzzles them until they make their escape. Pepper uses this trait as part of her security system.
