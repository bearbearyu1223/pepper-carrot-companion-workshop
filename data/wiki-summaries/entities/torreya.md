Torreya is a young witch of Ah from the generations of students that arrived at the school after the Great War. She is about nineteen or twenty and serves as the pilot of the dragon Arra. She showed an aptitude early on for communicating with the dragons of Ah, and her allegiance is more to the dragons than to the school itself; she endures her Ah training mainly because it allows her to spend time with them. Torreya is Shichimi's girlfriend.
