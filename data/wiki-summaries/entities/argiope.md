Argiope spiders are yellow pack-hunting arachnids primarily found in the Haunted Jungle, recognizable by a grayish spiral on their thorax or abdomen. They are one of the few spider species on Hereva that hunt in packs, using an intricate system of webbing, signals, and gestures to capture prey, and a remarkably developed hierarchy still being studied by Hereva's scientists. What looks like a small batch of curious spiders may be a scouting party watching territory and signaling for reinforcements. Disrupting that hierarchy is the only known way to deter them. They congregate around the red berry trees because their prey cannot resist the berries.
