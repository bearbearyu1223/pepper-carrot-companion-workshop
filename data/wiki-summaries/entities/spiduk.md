Spidük is a Chaosah demon who looks much like a large purple spider. She is skilled at Chaosah magic but, like Eyeük, lacks the ability to travel between universes; unlike Eyeük, this restriction does not bother her. She is content in her service to the Chaosah witches. On the battlefield she attacks several opponents at once using her many arms and her webbing, and she has mastered Chaosah battle magic that lets her alter reality to her choosing. She appreciates the finer things in life and will pause her battle rages for a nice cup of tea or a pastry before resuming the destruction of her enemies.
