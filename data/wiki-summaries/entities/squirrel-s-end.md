Squirrel's End is a small farming village of around two thousand people tucked into a fertile clearing of the dense forest that surrounds it. Settlers chose the spot for its quiet and built the village up around lumber from the forest. It is part of the lands of King Acren, though his waning influence means most villagers only know his name from sporadic taxes. The villagers concentrate on self-sufficiency, working the fields with diluted forms of Hippiah magic.

The surrounding forest houses many dangerous creatures, so most villagers stay out and trust their trap-making skills to defend the village. The folk colors are a red tartan, which Pepper wears on her arms and legs, and white; both signify pride in the hard labor that keeps the village thriving in adverse conditions. The School of Hippiah Magic stands on the outskirts of the village, drawing students from across Hereva.

Pepper was raised at the Little Acorns Orphanage in Squirrel's End, where she showed her magical aptitude and was first enrolled in Hippiah. A photograph of her winning the first potion contest in Komona is displayed in a small case near the orphanage entrance, making her the most famous, or infamous, alumna. Pepper's house sits in the forest outside the village, where she lives with Carrot and the three Chaosah witches; the original Chaosah house has been rebuilt many times after various magical accidents. The mountains behind Squirrel's End are home to several of the security creatures Pepper has recruited to keep intruders away, including Theorem the Golem, the Dragonmoose, and Old-Sylvan.
