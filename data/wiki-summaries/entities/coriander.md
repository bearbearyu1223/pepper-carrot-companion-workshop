Coriander is a young Zombiah witch and the Princess, later the Queen, of Qualicity, the large industrial city of the Technologist-Union. She is about eighteen and lives in a castle near the Technologist-Union headquarters, where she would rather tinker with machinery in her laboratory than perform her royal duties. Her familiar is Mango, a black cockerel rumored to have grand designs of his own. Her teacher is Soumbala, the robot master of Zombiah.

Coriander's themes are industry, voodoo, necromancy in the sense of animating non-living beings, robotics, and making things by hand. Her stated ambition is to invent a new kind of magic grounded in logical and physical law so that all people, not just witches, can use it. She is heir to the school's tradition through her grandmother Apiaceae, who founded Zombiah and once ruled Qualicity. Coriander's distinctive outfit and hat belonged to Apiaceae when she was young.

Coriander's known spells include the Potion of Zombification she introduced at the first Komonan potion contest and a skeleton-summoning spell she has used in later contests. Her coronation took place at the Cathedral of Qualicity and involved a small diplomatic incident over whether automatons or human staff should serve guests; Ah threatened to refuse to attend if automatons served, and the compromise was that Magmah brought its own catering staff. Coriander is happier alone with a wrench in hand, but she takes her duties seriously enough when they cannot be deferred. She is one of Pepper's closest friends and shares the affectionate rivalry of the four young witches that includes Pepper, Saffron, and Shichimi.
