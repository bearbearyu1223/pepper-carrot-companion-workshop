Prince Acren is the young, as-yet-uncrowned king of Acren, orphaned after the death of his parents the King and Queen of Acren. He is about eighteen, and was fourteen when first impersonated by Thyme in episode 11. His kingdom is the land in which Squirrel's End sits, though his rule has waned to the point that most villagers know his name only from the sporadic taxes his administration collects.

His waning influence is also why Komona considers itself outside his jurisdiction. The flying city floats above his lands and refuses both his taxation and his rule. Acren has been unable to subjugate Komona because his armies are land-based units untrained for airborne combat, which makes Komona the most desirable prize that is literally beyond his reach. A more effective king might try to bring the city to heel; for now the standoff persists.
