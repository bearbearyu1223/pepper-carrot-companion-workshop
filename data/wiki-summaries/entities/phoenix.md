The Phoenix is a large magical bird made of magma and fire, with long feathers on the top of its head and a shining brilliance. Phoenixes are near-immortal through their regenerative powers: at the end of life they return to the Valley of Volcanoes, where the magma they are made of consumes the body and the resulting ashes fall back into the volcano to reform the bird. Phoenixes in captivity do not thrive because they cannot regenerate; would-be captors are left with inert ashes and charred cages. Phoenix tears cure Rea poisoning, but a phoenix will not give up its tears willingly, challenging supplicants to earn them.
