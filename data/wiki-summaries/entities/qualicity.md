Qualicity is a large industrial city standing alone in the middle of a desert within the Technologist-Union. The city is famous for its mechanical work, manufacturing clockwork gadgets and automatons that staff the factories and perform much of the daily labor. The few human residents either maintain the mechanical constructs or handle other city maintenance. Qualicity stands on giant pillars over a dug-out cavern; an early generation of mining automatons stripped the surrounding ground so efficiently that the city was nearly lost, and the chasm became, in the King's words, a feature rather than the fault of bugs.

Zombiah is the predominant school in Qualicity, with its treaty-bound use of the Zombification Process. Coriander, the city's queen and the most famous Zombiah practitioner, tinkers in her castle laboratory near the Technologist-Union headquarters. The Cathedral of Qualicity is the city's central religious structure, where its monarchs are crowned when the moons of Hereva align favorably.
