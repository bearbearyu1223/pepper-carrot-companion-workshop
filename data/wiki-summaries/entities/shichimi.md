Shichimi is a young witch of Ah and one of Pepper's closest friends. She is about eighteen, lives a nomadic life in the Land of the Setting Moons where the three moons of Hereva sink to the horizon, and travels with her familiar Yuzu, a two-tailed orange kitsune. Her clothing is a simple robe and wrap similar to a kimono, and she wears a hairpiece fashioned from the bone of one of the dragons near the Temples of Ah; what it signifies and how she came by it is known only to her.

Shichimi was selected by Ah itself, which is how most members of the school come into the fold. She is themed around other worlds, mysticism, ghosts, regeneration, growth, and energy, and her stated motivation is to live a quiet life and die in peace, guided by the unknowable winds. Despite her young age she is one of the Knights of Ah, the most secretive and exclusive level of the school; most knights require decades of service to be deemed worthy of the honor.

Shichimi is observant and aware of her place in the wider current of life, in keeping with the school of Ah's spiritual focus. Her known spells include the Potion of Giant Monster she contributed to the disastrous first potion contest in Komona, and a blinding light spell. Her rivalry with Pepper, Coriander, and Saffron at magic contests is competitive but affectionate; the four young witches function more as a friend group than as adversaries. Her partner is Torreya, a dragon rider of Ah.
