Pepper is the protagonist of Pepper&Carrot, a young witch of Chaosah magic with dark red hair, pale skin, and an enormous pointed black hat. She lives in the original Chaosah house in the forest near Squirrel's End, raised by her three godmothers Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin after the orphans of Little Acorns enrolled her in the Hippiah school. When her frustration with Hippiah's flower-growing exercises produced a frustrated blast of Rea, the remaining Chaosah witches recognized their new heir and brought her home.

She is the sole living member of Chaosah; her godmothers were resurrected by Zombiah after dying in the Great War, which makes Pepper their legal heir and the formal owner of the house. Her magic specializes in particles, gravity, time paradox, and space deformation, summoning Chaosah demons or opening doors to micro dimensions more readily than she can cast a fire spell or heal a wound. Her wand-hand is right because Chicory the founder was right-handed, even though Pepper herself is left-handed.

Pepper is generous, fiercely loyal to her friends, and quick to help neighbors who would often prefer she didn't. Her magic skews dramatic and high-energy: potions that explode, summons that go interestingly wrong, and fixes that arrive at exactly the last possible moment. Her signature spells include the Potion of Flight, the Laughing Potion, the Mega-hair-growth Potion, summoning the three demons of Chaosah, and conjuring small Chaosah black holes. Being a witch of Chaosah pays poorly, and Pepper has rarely had Ko to spare for ingredients until her win at the first Komonan potion contest gave her a brief financial cushion.
