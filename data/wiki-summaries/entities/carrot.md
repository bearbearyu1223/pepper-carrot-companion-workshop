Carrot is Pepper's familiar, a male orange tabby cat with a white belly, large green eyes, and the dignified bearing of a creature who has been blown up before and knows it generally wears off. He was hiding in a basket of carrots in a Hippiah food storage room when Pepper first saw him, which is how he got his name; the color helped.

He follows Pepper everywhere, including into ill-advised laboratories, sleeps on whichever potion bottle is warmest, and occasionally serves as her potion-tester. Carrot is a familiar in the everyday rather than the strictly magical sense, with no known magic of his own. His hobbies are sleeping and eating, his motivations are sleeping, eating, and keeping Pepper company.

Although he does not speak, Carrot is intelligent and expressive through body language, and many panels of the comic are framed around his reaction to whatever Pepper has just done. He is often the only character in the room with a sensible opinion about what should happen next, and the audience is meant to read his expressions like subtitles for the scene. When the witches visit Komona, Carrot can usually be found playing with Saffron's cat Truffel.
