Chaosah is the school of chaos magic and the base from which every other magic on Hereva flows. It is the house of time, gravity, particle physics, and underground divine forces, and some Chaosah scholars argue that the school's magic quietly shapes every other school's work. Chaosah values practical magic over flashy displays, and its practitioners work in closed quarters, reluctant to share secrets with outsiders. Chaosah magic can alter the shape and size of living beings and even open portals, called black holes, into worlds outside Hereva.

Chaosah was founded by a young witch named Chicory, who discovered the Chaosah Black Hole spell while tinkering one night. As the school grew, Chicory appointed apprentices; one was lost forever in a potion-rack accident, leading to the still-honored rule that a true witch of Chaosah does not climb the potion shelves. Thyme eventually took over the school, and Chicory secluded herself in a pocket dimension to continue her research undisturbed. The school's habit of burying failed experiments traces back to Chicory; this practice has had unintended consequences, since even failed Chaosah experiments can be powerful.

During the Great War every member of Chaosah was wiped out by the forces of Ah, which destabilized Hereva so severely that the Great Tree of Komona perished and the city began to fall. Recognizing Chaosah as integral to the world's magic, Ah recruited Zombiah to resurrect Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin on the condition that they produce an heir without continuing the school themselves. Pepper is that heir, and the present-day Chaosah operates from Chicory's original house in the forest of Squirrel's End. A true witch of Chaosah is mean, commands fear and obedience, does not use magic for daily chores, and treats a wanted poster as the sign of a good reputation.
