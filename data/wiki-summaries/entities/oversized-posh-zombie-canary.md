The Oversized-Posh-Zombie-Canary is the unfortunate result of the first magical potion contest in Komona. The canary was first resurrected by Coriander using a Zombiah potion, then made posh by Saffron's Potion of Poshness. Shichimi realized too late that her own contribution, a potion of extreme growth, would compound the problem; Saffron mistook her hesitation for modesty and poured the whole batch on the posh zombified bird. The result fluttered about wildly, drew the Komonan Guard, and was eventually pacified by one of Pepper's unconventional potions. The bird was last seen sulking off to build a nest, which she guards with quiet dignity.
