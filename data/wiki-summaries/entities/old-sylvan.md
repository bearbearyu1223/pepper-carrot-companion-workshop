Old-Sylvan is a sentient tree that lives in the forest of Squirrel's End as part of Pepper's security system. He stands guard, waiting patiently for intruders, and will entangle them in the vines that grow among his branches if necessary. He uses two branches as arms and is happy to chat at length with anyone who happens by, though he takes his guard post seriously. Several families of squirrels live inside him and pop out of his mouth from time to time to check for trouble; they are curious and excellent at spotting it.
