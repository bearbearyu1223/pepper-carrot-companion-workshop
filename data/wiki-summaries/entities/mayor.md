Mayor Bramble is the seventieth Mayor of Komona, in his late forties, and the highest office in the flying city. His administration came to prominence on campaign promises to restore Komona as Hereva's center of commerce and magical prowess. The flagship of that program was instituting an annual potion contest to surface the brightest magical talent across Hereva. The first contest, in episode 6, ended so badly for the residents of Komona that whether the contest will return and whether Bramble will be re-elected are both open questions. Even Bramble himself has reportedly had trouble getting past the checkpoint into Cerberus, the gated community above the city.
