Eyeük is the leader of the Chaosah demons. He has red fur, four eyes, and three horns on his head, and he wields a trident. As leader he thinks more strategically than the other demons, using his eyes to survey the lurking dangers ahead and his trident to spear opposition before they can react. In combat he slows time around himself to react more quickly. He can cast Chaosah spells but has been locked away from traversing into other universes unless summoned, a restriction he is desperate to break; the wise summoner is careful with him.
