Mango is Coriander's familiar, a black cockerel who is rarely far from her side in the castle near the Technologist-Union headquarters in Qualicity. He has no known magic, and like the other familiars he does not speak. His official motivation is secretly to rule the world, though he has not yet drawn up concrete plans for how to achieve this. Coriander's grandmother Apiaceae was the founder of Zombiah, and Mango has the patience of a creature who knows the household well.
