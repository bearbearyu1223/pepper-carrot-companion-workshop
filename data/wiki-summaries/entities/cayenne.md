Cayenne is the tall, thin, and rigid Chaosah witch who serves as Pepper's spell-casting tutor and one of her three godmothers. She is in her sixties and the most visible and foreboding of the surviving Chaosah witches. She commands respect and takes orders from no one except Thyme, whose head-of-school position she has dedicated her life to protecting.

During the Great War, Cayenne was the most feared witch of Chaosah, casting spells and causing devastation wherever she roamed. Little is known of her past, which suits her fine. She can often be seen chewing on a piece of grass to focus herself on the present, and she has perfected the intimidating dark stare for which Chaosah witches are known. Her known spells include throwing fire with her eyes, spell cancellation, and reducing the Chaosah house into its component particles.

Cayenne does not suffer Pepper's antics and potion-making lightly, often chastising her about what a true witch of Chaosah should be doing. She teaches Pepper the discipline of spell-casting where Cumin teaches potions and ingredients. Some Chaosah witches have speculated that, had Cumin not been Thyme's apprentice first, Cayenne would have taken the position. Like Thyme and Cumin she was resurrected by Zombiah after the Great War on the condition that she train an heir; Pepper is that heir. The wisdom she relays through Pepper includes the advice that a good dark stare is worth a dozen curses and that a true witch should stare opponents down and dominate them.
