Ah is the school of the after-life, ghosts, souls, and parallel universes, sitting at the top-left of the Impossible Triangle as the last sub-school of Evolutions. Because Ah deals with spirits and other planes of existence, its secrets are carefully guarded and passed only through oral tradition to trusted adepts. The school selects its own members; outsiders rarely learn its magic. Practitioners travel in small nomadic bands wearing red robes with decorative white-bone hairpieces, located primarily in the Land of the Setting Moons where the three moons of Hereva sink to the horizon.

Ah is led by Wasabi, who is permitted to wear distinctive white robes signifying her connection with the spirit world. Her predecessor Sansevieria founded the school after visions of helpful spirits, and was later pushed out by Wasabi's stricter teaching of spiritual purity. The most secretive level of Ah is its Knights, with Shichimi the only Knight to earn the rank at a young age. Torreya is one of Ah's dragon riders.

Ah entered the Great War late and decisively, angered by Zombiah's mass resurrections and Chaosah's expanding chaos. Its spirit warriors broke Zombiah and ultimately destroyed every member of Chaosah; the resulting magical imbalance forced Ah itself to authorize the resurrection of three Chaosah witches to restore balance. The treaties Ah brokered with the other schools after the war give it leverage that persists today.
