Apiaceae is the founder of Zombiah magic, a former Queen of Qualicity, the chief engineer behind the city's clockwork transformation, the creator of the robot Soumbala, and Coriander's grandmother. She discovered the Zombification Process while trying to resurrect her little mouse Anis, and founded Zombiah to develop the process fully. She mixed magic and technology to drive significant progress in Hereva, founded the Technologist-Union, and contributed to many structures including the Cathedral of Qualicity. She negotiated the post-war treaty with Ah that limited the Zombification Process on living beings. Coriander's distinctive outfit and hat belonged to Apiaceae when she was young.
