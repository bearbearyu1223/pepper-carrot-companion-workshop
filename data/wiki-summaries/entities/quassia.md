Quassia is a teacher at the School of Hippiah Magic. She is part-elf and spent many years running through forests and being around nature before joining Hippiah as a young witch. She teaches the classes on rapid plant growth, plant heartiness, shape, and vitality. She brings her students into the Hippiah courtyard to practice their growth spells on seeds; those who can grow their plants earn her approval, and those who can't draw her ire and sharp tongue. Despite her popularity she feels she understands plants better than the students do, and she aspires one day to lead Hippiah magic.
