Thyme is the small, calculating leader of the surviving witches of Chaosah and one of Pepper's three godmothers. She is presumed to be around 116 years old, though she tends to lie about her age. She rules the remaining witches with an arthritic but iron fist, and quietly schemes for Chaosah's restoration to the prominence it lost in the Great War.

Thyme tends to sneak off to a cave to consult The Web, where her sharp mind calculates the best path forward for her school. Her apprentice is Cumin, though Thyme is more comfortable taking counsel from Cayenne. She frequently bosses Cumin around and sends her on silly errands and dangerous quests with little regard for her well-being. Some Chaosah witches have wondered whether Thyme would prefer Cayenne as apprentice and whether those dangerous missions were meant to free up the position, since Chaosah apprenticeships are for life. Cumin nearly drowned on an errand to Aquah after Thyme failed to mention how far underwater the school is.

Thyme is one of three Chaosah witches resurrected by Zombiah after the Great War, on the condition that she train an heir and refrain from continuing the school herself; that heir is Pepper. Her known spells include transforming herself into Prince Acren and reorganizing particles to rebuild the Chaosah house after one of Pepper's accidents. She is also the person the innkeeper near Tenebrum reports to whenever a curious soul stumbles onto Chaosah's sacred mountain.
