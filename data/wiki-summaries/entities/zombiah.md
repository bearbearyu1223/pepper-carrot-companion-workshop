Zombiah is the school of animation and reanimation, sitting at the top-right of the Impossible Triangle as one of the three sub-schools of Evolutions. It sits at the intersection of the macabre, dealing with death, zombies, and darkness, and transformation, dealing with recycling, re-use, and animation. Zombiah is not specifically about raising the dead but about bringing out the innate movement in all things; it took centuries of economic strife before the school applied its magic to formerly living creatures.

Zombiah was founded by the sisters Apiaceae and Anemone in what would become Qualicity, when their childhood experiments with animating dolls grew into a way to ease their starving parents' workload. Their work scaled into the Technologist-Union and the city's clockwork economy. Apiaceae later resurrected her sister Anemone, the first Zombiah resurrection, and the practice has since been strictly limited by treaty with Ah; resurrections of living beings now require an Ah representative to be present.

Today most Zombiah work is on machines, not bodies, and the school's master is the wise robot Soumbala, created by Apiaceae herself. Coriander is its most famous student. As part of the school's treaty obligations resurrections may still be performed during contests and demonstrations, but larger-scale work is forbidden.
