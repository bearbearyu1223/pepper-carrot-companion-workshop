The Sage is the great and wise teacher of Hereva, located in the hot springs and content to stay there. He dispenses his wisdom to anyone who comes and listens, though most pilgrims report receiving little more than contented sighs and a promise that he will teach them when he gets out of the bath. Few have ever seen the Sage outside of his bath, and fewer still speak openly of his wisdom. The result is that he spends most of his time alone in the water, sighing with contentment, his wisdom unheard and unheeded by those who could not stand the temperature long enough.
