The Temples of Ah are located in the Land of the Setting Moons, where the three moons of Hereva enter Pink Moon before settling into the horizon. Under Wasabi's leadership the Temples have grown into a single monolithic walled castle and village fortified with gatehouses, battlements, and horned roofs that double as airborne defense. They serve both as centers of worship and as the central nervous system of the school of Ah, housing the trainees, the libraries of reports from wandering adepts, and Wasabi's combined office and living quarters.

Few outsiders have been inside. Visitors are given special green robes to distinguish them from the school's red-robed members. The land around the Temples is also the final resting place for many of Hereva's dragons, who come here to die in quiet contemplation; Ah honors them with a ceremony that releases the spirit and consumes the body in a fiery vortex, leaving only the bones used in the Temples' architecture and jewelry.
