The Dragon-Drake is one of the strange hybrids that emerged after the Great Tree of Komona ruptured and tore the magical fabric of Hereva. It has the head of a duck and the body of a small dragon, behaves more like a duck than a dragon, and can usually be found playfully soaring around the skies above Komona. About the size of an ordinary duck, with a long draconic tail, Dragon-Drakes are mostly harmless. They sometimes get attached to witches flying into Komona, only to be brushed aside when the witch needs to focus on the complex landing approach.
