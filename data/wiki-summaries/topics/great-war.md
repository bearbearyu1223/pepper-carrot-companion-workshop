The Great War broke out in the year 252 between Magmah and Aquah, after a series of escalating grievances about destructive weather patterns over Komona and Magmah witches draining Aquah's lakes. Chaosah, which had been growing quietly in strength, played both sides against each other and was largely responsible for tipping the dispute into open war. The conflict dragged in Zombiah after both sides discovered Zombiah was supplying weapons-materials to the other, and Hippiah caved from neutrality only to feed the starving.

The fighting escalated into the Elemental Battles, with Magmah and Aquah summoning fire and water elementals, Zombiah summoning earth elementals to defend itself, and Hippiah growing fortifications. Chaosah outdid them all by summoning the demons of Chaosah and tearing inter-dimensional holes in Hereva. This finally drew the High Council of Ah into the war in 254, alarmed by Zombiah's mass reanimation of fallen warriors and by Chaosah's holes threatening the integrity of Hereva itself. Ah went straight for Chaosah, systematically banishing the demons and destroying every Chaosah witch in turn.

When Cayenne and Thyme finally fell, Thyme's last spell drew so much Rea from the surroundings that the rupture killed both witches and shocked the Great Tree of Komona, which blew off a limb that became Kerberos. With Chaosah gone, the magic of Hereva began to deteriorate so badly that Ah was forced to enlist Zombiah to revive Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin to restore balance, with the standing condition that they train an heir without continuing the school themselves. The treaty Ah brokered with every other school is stored in a magic lock-box. Magmah and Aquah served their punishment by being renamed Kielbasah and Wharrgablah for ten years.
