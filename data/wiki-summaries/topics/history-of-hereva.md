Hereva's recorded history begins at year zero, the sprouting of the Great Tree of Komona. Events before that point are called the Before Times and are largely unknown; the dragons, who lived in Hereva long before humans, took no notice of humanity until the Great Tree appeared and humans started wielding magic. Several theories attempt to explain the simultaneous arrival of the tree, humans, and magic, but the dragons answer in riddles and the archaeological record is thin.

After the Great Tree the magical age began quickly. Spells were discovered, written down, and gathered into books, and the six schools formed as scholars specialized. For centuries the schools coexisted peacefully, with only Chaosah making serious trouble through its pranks. In the year 252 a brutal war broke out between Magmah and Aquah over weather and lake-drainage disputes, soon dragging in Zombiah, Hippiah, and finally Ah in 254. Chaosah, which had quietly stoked the conflict from the start, drew Ah's attention with its inter-dimensional spells and its summoning of demons, and Ah systematically banished the Chaosah demons and destroyed every Chaosah witch but Cayenne, Thyme, and Cumin. When even those three fell the Great Tree ruptured, blew off the branch that became Kerberos, and the city of Komona began to fall.

Ah enlisted Zombiah to revive the Chaosah witches and charged them with raising an heir, which is the role Pepper now occupies. The post-war treaties stripped each school of some powers and are stored in a magic lock-box that only select members can access. Wasabi of Ah keeps the surrendered war-spellbooks on display in the temples. Fourteen years later magic is in balance again, the schools are rebuilding, and Pepper carries the future of Chaosah on her shoulders.
